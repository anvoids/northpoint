Northgarden Park

Our town's main park offers scenic walking paths and a tranquil pond for residents and visitors to unwind and connect with nature. Located by the sea, it provides a perfect backdrop for serene moments of relaxation and beautiful waterfront views.

Northgarden Sea Promenade

This sea promenade extends from the park to the main bus terminal, providing a picturesque and leisurely route that allows you to enjoy the coastal beauty while conveniently connecting to transportation hubs.

Colorful Houses

As soon as you step in North Point, you see the 8 colorful houses greeting you. Each one uniquely painted to add a vibrant and welcoming touch to the town's charming streets. These cheerful facades symbolize the town's spirit of creativity and community.
